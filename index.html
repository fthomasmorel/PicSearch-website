Open PicSearch from iMessage

PicSearch lets you open an image search engine directly from your iMessage conversation thread.

  • Tap the "AppStore" logo.
  • Select PicSearch.
  • Then click on "Search".

Searching

With PicSearch, you just have to search for the picture you want using your own keywords.

  • Tap your keywords.
  • Select your image.
